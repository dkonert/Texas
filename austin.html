AUSTIN

2

Austin, the capital of Texas, was incorporated on December 27, 1839. Named after the cities founding fathers Stephen F. Austin. Located in Central Texas, Austin is known for its vibrant music scene, tech industry, and political significance as the state's political hub. As a major urban city, Austin is home to a diverse and rapidly growing population. The average income level in Austin tends to be higher than much of the state, driven by its booming tech sector and high concentration of skilled workers. It is often considered one of the most prosperous cities in Texas.
